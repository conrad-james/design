Loft extension, SW London


Our brief was for a major alteration to a family house in a London conservation area. The generous house extension consists of a new basement under part of the existing garden, a ground floor kitchen, dining and living area, a first floor extension and terrace, as well as various alterations to rationalise the existing layout.


Many design options were explored to achieve the clients vision and make the most of the site.


Abstracted 3D visulas were produced to illustrate the spatial qualities of the rooms without revealing the finishes, at this design stage.
